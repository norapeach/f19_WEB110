About Me

Watching a close friend edit Ruby programs and utilize Ruby on Rails while I was living in Dresden in 2012 was when I first felt the itch to learn to code. After working in Customer Support for a few tech companies since returning to Seattle in 2013, I'm finally satisfying this urge to peak behind the tech curtain via Seattle Central's Web Development Program.

Although I have some meager knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I'm eager to move past the basics and discover where my interests truly lie. Whether it's diving deeper into mobile or software development, I ultimately hope to find a way to leverage what I learn at SCC with my fluency in German.

Outside Interests

When I'm not sitting behind a screen, I prefer to be biking. My 1980's Novara is my favorite method of transportation and as long as there is no snow on the ground - at least in Seattle - it's biking season to me.

Since it's opening in 2015, I have been volunteering in the kitchen of Nordo's Culinarium - Pioneer Square's own immersive dinner theater.
