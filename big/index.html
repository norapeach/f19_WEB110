Welcome

This website provides a digital snapshot of my current status in the process of expanding my web development skill set. As of November 2019, I’ve included three research whitepapers that touch on key subjects for web designers and developers to consider. Additionally, I’ve implemented a number of standard website features on each of the pages in the navigation bar, such as a simple responsive Lightbox II Gallery, embedded Google tools as well as a functioning contact form.

Self-evaluating as a novice, I view being constantly confronted with what I do not yet know as an opportunity to dig in and learn. At this point I plan to build on the HTML and CSS that I have begun to utilize through additional projects, such as the examples that Chris Coyier suggests in the CSS-Tricks article that I’ve linked in the resources box. Although I feel like it’s still early for me to determine where my focus will land as I grow as a developer, I am drawn to JavaScript based on my amateur experience in self-study and want to dive in further and start adding self-coded JS features to my projects as soon as possible. Beyond that, I plan to continue learning Python and have started collecting a list of small programs that I want to attempt writing

Carving out time in my days to sit and write code is just one piece in my growth process. An equal amount of time can be spent reading what successful developers are writing in order to get moving on my path of continuous learning. Outside of this, I am eager to get to a point where I feel confident enough to connect with other developers via meetups - and someday - conferences.
